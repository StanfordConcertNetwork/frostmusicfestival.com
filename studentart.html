Showcase your art at Frost!


Stanford Concert Network's Frost Music and Arts Festival is an annual celebration of the musical and visual arts at Stanford. On Saturday, May 21, 2022, Frost will be held in person in Frost Amphitheater for the first time in two years.

Each year, the Frost student art exhibition is curated around an aspect of the headliners that inspires our Stanford community. This year we will have performances by Aminé, Tkay Maidza, and Victoria Monét, dynamic artists who bridge gaps between diverse aesthetics.

This year’s theme asks: How do we connect?
Connection is not just a compromise but sparks originality. Your art should speak to sites of connection that fuse different themes or experiences. As we return to Frost together, your installation should harbor a new site for connection.
