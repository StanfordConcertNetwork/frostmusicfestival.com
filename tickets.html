Stanford Student Tickets

Stanford student tickets go on pre-sale Thursday, March 15 at 10:00 AM. Like the Stanford Concert Network Facebook page to access the pre-sale. Remaining tickets go on sale on Friday, March 16 at 10:00 AM.

Purchase Stanford Student Tickets

Your Stanford student ticket will be pre-loaded to your Student ID.

Currently registered Stanford students are able to purchase 1 ticket at a reduced price.

Non-Stanford Guests with Stanford tickets will be responsible for paying the difference at the on-site Box Office.

Purchase Stanford Student Tickets

For Stanford ticketing issues, please call 1-(800)-STANFORD.

General Admission Tickets

General admission tickets go on pre-sale Thursday, March 15 at 10:00 AM with a special Goldenvoice special promo code. Remaining tickets go on sale on Friday, March 16 at 10:00 AM.

Purchase General Admission Tickets

For AXS ticketing issues, please call 1-888-929-7849.


We are excited to announce Glass Animals as this year’s headliner for the 7th Annual Frost Music and Arts Festival.

Additionally Raven Lenae and Monte Booker will be performing a special collaboration together.

This year's Frost student opener will be chosen by a Battle of the Bands. Artist of any genre are welcome to apply! To apply, fill out the form here! The deadline to apply is Friday 4/7 at 11:59 PM. Please contact frostmusicfestival@gmail.com if you have any questions.

Frost Amphitheater is still under renovations. We will be hosting this year’s Frost Music & Arts Festival in the Stanford Stadium again. Previous headliners include Zedd, Fetty Wap, Flume, Dispatch, MGMT, and Modest Mouse.

A limited number of pre-sale tickets will be available on Thursday, March 15 at 10:00 AM. Tickets will go on-sale to the Stanford student body and general admission tickets at 10:00 AM on Friday, March 16, 2018.

Discounted Stanford student tickets will be available through the Stanford Athletics website. Only 1 ticket will be available to Stanford students, and will be pre-loaded to your Stanford ID. All patrons are required to have a ticket. Stanford staff and faculty will receive a promo code in the Stanford Report via e-mail in the Friday, March 16 edition.

Regular priced tickets for general admission will be available on AXS.com. For questions or comments, please email frostmusicfestival@gmail.com or call (650) 725-3112.


Stanford Faculty/Staff/Alumni Tickets

Stanford Faculty/Staff will be able to buy up to 2 discounted tickets for themselves and a guest. A special link with a promo code can be found in the Stanford Report in the Friday, March 16 edition. Any other people that attempt to access these tickets will have their order canceled.

Stanford Alumni will be able to buy up to 2 discounted tickets for themselves and a guest. A special link with a promo code can be found in the Stanford Report in the Friday, March 16 edition, in addition to the Stanford Bay Area Alumni Facebook. Any other people that attempt to access these tickets will have their order canceled.
