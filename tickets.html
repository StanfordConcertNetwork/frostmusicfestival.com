Stanford Faculty/Staff/Alumni Tickets

Stanford Faculty/Staff are able to buy up to 2 discounted tickets for themselves and a guest with a special link that is in the Stanford Report in the Friday, April 7 edition. Any other people that attempt to access these tickets will have their order canceled.

Stanford Alumni are able to buy up to 2 discounted tickets for themselves and a guest with a special link that is in the Stanford Report in the Friday, April 7 edition, in addition to the Stanford Bay Area Alumni Facebook. Any other people that attempt to access these tickets will have their order canceled.


Ticket Levels

Three ticket levels are available for purchase:

  1. Field
  2. Lower Bleacher
  3. Upper Bleacher

Field tickets are available on a first-come first-serve basis. The same number of field tickets have been made available to both Stanford and General Admission ticket purchasers.
