About the Festival

Frost is Stanford University's annual music and arts festival. Frost has traditionally been held in Frost Amphitheater, but due to the pandemic is being held online this year. Despite this, we have an amazing lineup for you all, as well as an innovative online art gallery of student art. Attend an exciting performance from Phoebe Bridgers, Omar Apollo, Jamila Woods, Raveena, Samia, and student opener Linda Sol, and stick around for an after-party DJ set by Jai Wolf. We can't wait to see you there!
